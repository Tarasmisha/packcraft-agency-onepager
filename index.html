Platform Copy

Captions, hooks, and hashtags adapted for IG, TikTok, and LinkedIn. Includes specific hooks (e.g., “Niacinamide: Everyone says ‘you need it.’ No one says how much.”)

Visual Brief

Slide-by-slide direction for the designer. Includes hex codes, font specs, and line-art icon placement.

Video Script

Scene-by-scene structure with timing, voiceover, and on-screen text. Includes both face-to-camera and faceless/B-roll variants.

Execution Checklist

Role-specific handoffs and QA gates to validate content quality and technical safe zones (IG/TikTok) before publishing.
